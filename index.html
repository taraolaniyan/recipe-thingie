Ingredients:

  • Onions
  • Cameroonian Pepper/Black Pepper
  • Indomie Spice(That already came in the pack)
  • Crushed Pepper
  • Butter(sometimes)
  • Half a cube of Maggi
  • Any Type of Stew you have
  • Half a teaspoon of honey
  • A few drops of Soy Sauce

How i Make it

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. When it's bubbling a lot add the indomie into the pot
  3. Put the stove heat on low and chop some onions while it boils for a bit.
  4. When the noodles is a bit lighter than before add the noodles spice
  5. Then all the other spices and sauces.
  6. Cook the noodles to your preffered texture then pour the noodles into a plate
  7. Then.. ENJOY!!
